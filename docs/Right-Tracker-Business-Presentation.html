The Challenge We're Solving

Today, IT support requests are handled through emails, phone calls, and walk-ins. This leads to lost requests, zero visibility, and no way to measure performance.

Today (Without Right Tracker)

  • Requests via email, phone, walk-ins get lost
  • Employees don't know the status of their requests
  • No way to track who is working on what
  • Same issue raised multiple times by different people
  • No data on resolution times or team performance
  • IT team spends 30%+ time on status follow-ups
  • Management has zero visibility into IT operations
➔

Tomorrow (With Right Tracker)

  • Every request logged in one centralized system
  • Employees track their tickets in real-time
  • Full audit trail: who did what, when
  • Duplicate detection and ticket linking
  • Dashboard with resolution times and SLA compliance
  • Self-service portal eliminates manual follow-ups
  • Reports and analytics for data-driven decisions

What is Right Tracker?

A web-based Ticket Management System that centralizes all IT support requests into a single, trackable, and transparent workflow.

Security & Data Protection

Your data is safe. Right Tracker implements enterprise-grade security measures based on proven patterns from our existing projects.

🔒

Secure Login

Passwords hashed with bcrypt. JWT tokens with 24h expiry. Account lockout after failed attempts.

🛡

Role-Based Access

Three permission levels ensure users only see and do what they're authorized for. No data leakage.

💪

Attack Prevention

Rate limiting stops brute force. Input validation prevents injection. Security headers block XSS and clickjacking.

🔐

Encrypted in Transit

HTTPS enforced in production. All data encrypted between browser and server via TLS/SSL.

👁

Full Audit Trail

Every action logged — who changed what, when. Complete accountability for compliance.

📦

Isolated Deployment

Docker containers isolate each service. Database not exposed to the internet. Secrets managed via environment variables.
